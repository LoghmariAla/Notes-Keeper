Status of Palestine

Free Palestine

The call for a free Palestine is a fundamental demand for the right of the Palestinian people to live in freedom and self-determination, free from occupation and oppression.

This aspiration for freedom is at the heart of the Palestinian struggle, and it is essential for the international community to support this cause and work towards a just and lasting solution.
